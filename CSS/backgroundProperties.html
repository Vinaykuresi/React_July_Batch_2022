What are the 4 main flower types? Image result for types of flowers There are commonly four distinct whorls of flower parts: (1) an outer calyx consisting of sepals; within it lies (2) the corolla, consisting of petals; (3) the androecium, or group of stamens; and in the centre is (4) the gynoecium, consisting of the pistils.
  • Alstroemeria.
  • Calla Lily.
  • Daisy.
  • Gardenia.
  • Carnation.
  • Gerbera Daisy.
  • Orchid.
  • Tulip.
